Construction Map

Map Plotting

This is an architecture plan drawing, which shows the buildings,utility runs,and the position of roads, an existing/proposed project site at a defined scale.

House Designing

House designing is a drawing to scale, showing a view from above, of the relationships between rooms, spaces and other physical features at many levels of a structure.

About us

Mirza Mohd Akhtar was born in Allahabad, UP where he earned a Diploma in Architecture from Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology Allahabad. He first worked in the office of Rural Engineering Development (UP Government) serving as Civil Engineer for several projects across the state.

Mirza Mohd Akhtar is determined to continuously learn about a client’s business and how architecture can help them achieve their goals. The practices’ portfolio includes projects ranging from Road, Recreational, Educational and Residential architecture.

He is son of   Late. Mirza Habib Baig(Architecture Engineer)    who designed and build many schools such as BJS, BOYs High Scool etc in Allahabad.
